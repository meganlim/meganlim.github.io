GenEdit Research Intern

Prof. Niren Murthy Lab, UC Berkeley

GenEdit is a startup founded out of UC Berkeley's drug delivery Prof. Niren Murthy that focuses on solving 2 main problems with the current viral gene editing delivery approach: permanent off-site targeting effects and packaging of all CRISPR components. As an intern I performed transcardial perfusions on the mice in order to test efficiency of delivery vehicles and learned chemical formulation of the polymers that encompass the packaging system.
